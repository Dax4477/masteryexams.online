Lofi vs. Classical: Best Study Music?

Should you study in silence, or blast your favorite tunes? The answer depends on what you are studying.

The "Lyrics" Problem

If you are reading or writing, music with lyrics is a disaster. Your brain's language center is trying to process the song words and your textbook words at the same time. This drops IQ by up to 10 points.

Why Video Game Music Works

Soundtracks for games (like Zelda or Halo) are specifically designed to keep you focused without being distracting. They are excellent for repetitive tasks like math problems.

Binaural Beats

Some studies suggest that 40Hz binaural beats can help improve concentration, but the best "sound" for deep work is usually simple white noise or rain sounds.
