How to Manage Time During Online Exams

The number one reason students fail isn't lack of knowledge—it's running out of time. Here is the strategy to pace yourself perfectly.

1. The "First Scan" Rule

Never start answering immediately. Spend the first 2 minutes scanning the entire paper. This helps your brain prioritize easy questions and mentally prepare for the hard ones.

2. The 1-Minute Rule

If you are stuck on a multiple-choice question for more than 60 seconds, flag it and move on. It is better to answer 3 easy questions in the next 3 minutes than to solve 1 hard question and run out of time.

3. Simulate Reality

Practicing without a timer is useless. You must train your brain to work under pressure.

4. Save 10 Minutes for Review

Always aim to finish the exam 10 minutes early. Use this time to check for silly mistakes, not to second-guess your gut instincts on hard questions.
